Background

During my time at CMU, I worked as a Teaching Assistant for several courses including Algorithm Design and Analysis, Probability and Computing, and Great Ideas in Theoretical Computer Science.

Teaching was incredibly rewarding, and the skills I developed helping students understand complex topics have proven valuable in mentoring new engineers in my professional career.
